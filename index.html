Karachi – City of Lights

Overview

Karachi is the largest city of Pakistan and the backbone of its economy. It is a coastal megacity known for diversity, ports, business hubs, and nightlife.

History

Karachi developed from a small fishing village into a major port during British rule. After 1947, it became Pakistan’s first capital and rapidly expanded.

Culture & Food

Karachi offers foods from all over Pakistan including Biryani, Nihari, Street BBQ, and international cuisines.

Economy & Education

Karachi contributes the largest share to Pakistan’s GDP and hosts major universities, banks, and industries.
